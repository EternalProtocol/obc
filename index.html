ETH1 Labs Limited is a Web3 technology team focused on sovereign blockchain infrastructure, with its compliant entity registered in Hong Kong, an international financial center. The company is committed to building a next-generation digital economy system centered on user sovereignty, promoting the compliant application and large-scale deployment of blockchain technology globally.

ETH1 Labs focuses on three core areas: sovereign Layer2 public chains, smart wallets, and on-chain social networks, building a comprehensive solution that integrates asset management, digital identity systems, governance mechanisms, and consensus propagation, serving Web3 users, developers, and global asset on-chain and circulation needs.

In terms of compliance, VEIL LABS has obtained a U.S. MSB (Money Services Business) license related to digital currencies, providing the foundation for conducting compliant digital asset services.
